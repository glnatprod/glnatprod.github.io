Goals of the Symposium

The Great Lakes Natural Product Symposium will be held at Queen’s University (Kingston, Ontario) on May 4th – 5th , 2019. The goal of the symposium is to bring together students in the field of natural product discovery to develop a strong network for collaboration while learning about exciting new research.

Researchers from each lab will give a presentation on research efforts and the session will include a plenary lecture. A poster session will also be held allowing all attendees to present and discuss their research.

Our keynote speaker for 2019 is Dr. Sean Brady from The Rockefeller University, New York City.
